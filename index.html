About Handyman Harry

Handyman Harry is a privately owned business here to help you with all the things you can't do or don't have time to do. Handyman Harry pride themselves on providing the best level of service to you the customer. With a reputation for unparalleled customer service, quality work, brilliant communications, honest work ethic all being carried out with old school values. Handyman Harry provides renovation and maintenance to all domestic and body corporate properties within the Melbourne bayside area. Please contact Handyman Harry where you will receive a happy friendly service for any jobs you need doing.
